I love QGIS, but I would admit some of the features are still less intuitive for me to use. I think I'll get there though. This is perhaps one of the most complicated maps I have ever made. It may not look it, but it wasn't as easy as other maps I have made previously. Some internal calculations here, API key use there, I finally produced what you are looking at.

I decided to recreate this Bloomberg map on a different state - Georgia. To make it easier to understand, I limited my variables (layers), to just two; vaccination sites and location of within 10 mins drive of dollar stores.

Georgia has 411 vaccination sites and over 900 dollar stores.
